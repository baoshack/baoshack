Hiring

Bao Shack at Trailside in Mount Stewart specializes in traditional Taiwanese food. Since Bao Shack is only open 10 days in the entire year, we are looking for a highly motivated individual to help create a memorable experience for our customers. Previous experience working in the service industry is an asset.

Job Location: Trailside in Mount Stewart, Prince Edward Island

Work Term: Every Saturday from June 29th to August 31st

Schedule: Lunch shift from 11am to 2pm & dinner shift from 5pm to 7pm

Salary: $30 per hour



Key Responsibilities:

  • Assist customers with orders at the POS
  • Handle sales transactions, cash and credit card processing, and reconcile the register
  • Make boba drinks and coffee
  • Assist with distributing orders in the dining area
  • Must be able to work in a fast paced, dynamic environment
  • Other duties as necessary


Please send your resume to baoshack@gmail.com
