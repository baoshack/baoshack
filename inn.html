Trailside Inn

Trailside Inn is located above Bao Shack restaurant. There will be a total of four private suites with its own entrance and washroom. You can view the photos of the rooms at AirBnB Cherry Hill (queen bed), Apple Valley (two twin beds), Savage Harbour (queen bed) and Blooming Point (queen bed). For booking, you can book via AirBnB (more expensive) or with us directlty (no AirBnB booking fee).

For 2023, price is $129 plus HST per room per night.

To make a reservation with us directly, please email baoshack@gmail.com. Once we confirm the availability, please submit the full payment via etransfer in Canadian funds along with the driver's license to baoshack@gmail.com to reserve the room

Below is our cancellation policy:
  • 7 days prior to the check-in date: 100% refund
  • 2 days prior to the check-in date: 1 night stay will be deducted from the refund
  • less than 2 days from the check-in date: 1 night stay plus 50% of unspent stays will be deducted from the refund
